Employment

In order to join the ranks of the RANDOMDERPS you must be able to win a competitive CSGO match using only a pistol.

In order for the match to be valid, there must be a RANDOMDERPS proctor on the team.

RANDOMDERPS proctors are ranked moderator or above.

Positions available

RANDOMDERP member

Description: Be able to play with us when we are on.

Requirements: Win a competitive CSGO match with only a pistol with a RANDOMDERPS proctor in the match.

Proctor (moderator and above)

Description: Allowed to approve of members.

Requirements: must be specifically selected by the RANDOMDERPS founders.
